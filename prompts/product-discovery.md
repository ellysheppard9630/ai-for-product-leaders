# Product Discovery Prompts

Reusable AI prompts for product discovery, research synthesis, and opportunity framing.

## Opportunity Framing Prompt

Help me analyze this product opportunity:

Context:
[Insert business/customer context]

Customer Problem:
[Insert problem]

Goal:
[Insert desired outcome]

Please provide:
1. Key assumptions
2. Risks
3. Discovery questions
4. Success metrics
5. Recommended next steps
